Tuna Pasta

A delicious spaghetti meal with tuna.

This recipe is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Tuna
  • Lemon
  • Anchovies
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add garlic and anchovies
  2. Boil spaghetti in a pot
  3. Add spaghetti to the oil pan
  4. Mix in tuna
  5. Add some lemon juice to taste
