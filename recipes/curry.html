Curry

A curry dish gauranteed to please your family.

This recipe is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Curry Roux
Steps:
  1. Cut the onions, potatos, and carrots
  2. Cook the onions until brown
  3. Add Chicken and cook until the sides are not pink
  4. Add boiling water
  5. Add the carrots and Potatos and let simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Mix in the Curry Roux
  7. Serve with rice and enjoy
