Tomato Pasta

A classic tomato spaghetti recipe.

Simple yet perfect.

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Can of Tomatos
  • Ancovies
  • Butter
Steps:
  1. Heat oil in a pan and add anchovies
  2. Add the can of tomatos into the pan
  3. Add 50g of butter and let simmer for 45 minutes
  4. Boil spaghetti in a pot
  5. Add cooked shaghetti to the tomato sauce and serve
